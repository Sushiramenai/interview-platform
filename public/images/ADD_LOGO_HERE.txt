ADDING YOUR SENBIRDTEA LOGO
============================

To add your logo to the platform:

1. Save your logo image file as one of these names:
   - logo.png (recommended)
   - logo.svg (if you have vector format)
   - logo.jpg

2. Place the logo file in this directory:
   /public/images/

3. The logo will automatically appear in:
   - HR Dashboard header
   - Interview room (top-left corner)
   - Results page header

Logo specifications:
- Recommended size: 200x200px minimum
- Transparent background preferred (PNG or SVG)
- The platform will automatically resize it

The logo is already integrated into the design with your brand colors!
